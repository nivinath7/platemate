# Platemate 🥗

Platemate is an AI-powered nutrition assistant that lets you 
upload your personal meal plan and ask questions about your 
diet, macros, and food choices — like having a nutritionist 
in your pocket.

Most meal planning apps give you generic plans that ignore 
where you live, what you can afford, and what you actually 
like to eat. Platemate works with YOUR plan, in YOUR context.
